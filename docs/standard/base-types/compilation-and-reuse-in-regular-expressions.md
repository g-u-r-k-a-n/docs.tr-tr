 <span data-ttu-id="8b848-123">Önbelleğe alınan en fazla normal ifade sayısı, `static` (`Shared` Visual Basic) <xref:System.Text.RegularExpressions.Regex.CacheSize%2A?displayProperty=nameWithType> özelliğindeki değere göre belirlenir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b848-123">The maximum number of cached regular expressions is determined by the value of the `static` (`Shared` in Visual Basic) <xref:System.Text.RegularExpressions.Regex.CacheSize%2A?displayProperty=nameWithType> property.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8b848-124">Varsayılan olarak, normal ifade altyapısı, en fazla 15 derlenmiş normal ifadeyi önbelleğe alır.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b848-124">By default, the regular expression engine caches up to 15 compiled regular expressions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8b848-125">Derlenmiş normal ifadelerin sayısı önbellek boyutunu aşarsa, en az kullanılan normal ifade atılır ve yeni normal ifade önbelleğe alınır.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b848-125">If the number of compiled regular expressions exceeds the cache size, the least recently used regular expression is discarded and the new regular expression is cached.</span></span>
